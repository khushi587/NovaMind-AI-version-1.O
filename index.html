Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is NovaMind a chatbot?

No. NovaMind doesn't generate conversation for its own sake — it helps you organize, connect, and reflect on your own ideas.

Do I own my notes and data?

Yes, always. Your notes belong to you and can be exported at any time in plain text.

Can I use NovaMind for academic research?

It's built for exactly that — long-form research, citation tracking, and connecting ideas across many sources.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan includes up to 200 notes and a basic knowledge graph, no credit card required.

Can teams collaborate inside NovaMind?

The Teams plan adds shared workspaces and a team-wide knowledge graph for collaborative research.
